Authentic Ayurveda

Ayurveda, which is supposed to have originated over 6000 years ago, recorded in ancient scriptures and passed down generations, is the oldest surviving holistic science of healing and wellness.The word 'Ayur-veda’ is derived from the ancient Sanskrit words ‘ayu' meaning 'life' and ‘veda' meaning 'knowledge'. This science or knowledge of healing, as mentioned in the Rig Veda, was revealed to Rishi Bharadvaja from the great Cosmic Intelligence. It was expounded and practiced by the same rishis, who were the propagators of Vedic civilisation in India, by organising the fundamentals of life into proper systems.
