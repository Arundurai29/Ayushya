Packages

Ayushya Ayurvedic Panchakarma Centre offers specialized Ayurvedic Health & Wellness Treatment Packages. We have a team of qualified physicians, well-trained masseurs and support staff to ensure that you receive authentic ayurvedic treatment for holistic healing.

Panchakarma

12 to 48 days - as cycles of 6 days (one to four cycles)

It is a process of purifying the whole body to attain proper balance of Vatha, Pitha and Kapha based on Panchakarma treatment in our traditional way. This package includes Body Massage, Sirodhara, Snehapanam, Vamanam, Virechanam, BrahmanaVasthi, Dhara, Pizhichil, Ghavarakizhi, Tharpanam, Karnapooranam, Body Pack, Anjanam, Gandoosham and Medicated bath. Each person will have a one-to-one consultation with the physician, and accordingly the treatment procedures will be prescribed.

Body Purification Therapy

Shodana Chikilsa - 12 days

It is a process of purifying the whole body to attain balance of Vatha, Pitha and Kapha by Panchakarma and Swetakarma treatment. This will also purify the gunas and doshas of mind viz. Satwa, Rajas and Thamas. The 12-day package process includes body massage, Snehapanam, Nasyam, SnehaVasthi, KashayaVasthi.

Special Treatment For Spine

This is a special package (15 Days) for your spine problems. It will strengthen the back muscles and prevent degeneration of bones and it will also prevent nerve compression. This is very good for spondylosis, post traumatic problems (post accidental), sports injuries etc. This treatment includes Body Massage, Snehapanam, Virechanam, Kadivasthi, SnehaVasthi, KashayaVasthi, Kizhi etc.

Body Immunisation / Anti-Aging Treatment

This is a prime treatment (18 Days) in Ayurveda for reducing aging process, arresting the degeneration of body cells, and immunizing the body system. Giving Rasayana (very special ayurvedic medicine) internally is a main part of this programme, based on Panchakarma and Swetakarma treatment. This package includes Body Massage, Snehapanam, Virechanam, Nasyam, Sneha Vasthi, Kashayavasthi, Pizhichil, Ghavarakizhi, Tharpanam, Sirovasthi, Anjanam, Karnapooranam and Medicated bath.

Slimming Programme

This programme (24 Days) includes massage, in take of herbal juice, herbal preparations as advised during this period. There are two steps of massage.

  • Medicated herbal power massage
  • Medicated herbal oil massage
